Our Mission

The reason why we dey give you this training

For code9ja, we get mind to provide coding education wey everybody fit get chance to access and we go make sure say the education wey we give na correct one wey go fit help young Nigerians and Africans all over the world. We believe say coding na one of the important things wey go help make person get better opportunity, creativity and e go even help bring innovation. Our main aim na to make sure say we carry everybody along and make the education wey we give affordable and e go easy for people wey no even sabi anything about coding or people wey don sabi small. We go make sure say we create one good learning environment wey go make all our students fit connect, collaborate and even grow together. We go make sure say we give our students the skills, mindset and confidence wey dem go take survive and excel for this digital world wey e dey evolve fast fast. Trust us, we no fit fall your hand.
